The McGill Engineering Ice Cream Store

Welcome to Frostbite Ice Cream

Frostbite is a student-run ice cream store at McGill University in Montreal, Canada.
We are known for our hype tracks, dope employees, and of course, our delicious ice cream.

We are located in the McConell Engineering Building, inside the EUS Mall.

Free Ice Cream

Got less than 30% on a midterm weighted more than 15%? It's your lucky day! FREE ice cream!

Toonie Tuesday

Join us on Tuesdays for Toonie Tuesday! A baby cup for only $2!

Bring Your Own Cup

Bring a reusable cup to get a deal of $1.50 per scoop!

Reward Cards

Pick up a card in-store to receive a free ice cream reward with every 5 ice cream treats purchased!
